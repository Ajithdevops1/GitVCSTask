Code of Conduct

Introduction

Our community is dedicated to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for everyone. This code of conduct outlines our expectations for participants and consequences for unacceptable behavior.

Guidelines

  • Be respectful of others, their ideas, and their experiences.
  • Avoid demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech.
  • Listen actively and seek to understand differing perspectives.

Unacceptable Behavior

  • Harassment of any kind, including offensive comments or threats.
  • Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
  • Disruption of discussions, talks, or other activities.

Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to our moderation team at conduct@example.com.

Enforcement

Participants who are asked to stop any inappropriate behavior are expected to comply immediately. Repeated or serious violations may result in sanctions, up to and including a ban from the community.
